I do not provide account registration, comments, or direct user profiles on this website.

Data may be processed for the following purposes:

- to detect technical issues and measure general traffic trends.

## Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics helps me understand how visitors use the website by collecting information such as:

- pages visited,
- approximate location,
- device and browser information,
- referral source,
- interactions and session statistics.

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar identifiers to recognize a browser or device and measure website usage over time.

Google may process this information on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States. More information about how Google processes data is available in Google's own documentation and privacy materials.

You can limit Google Analytics tracking by:

- blocking or deleting cookies in your browser settings,
- using browser tools that limit tracking technologies,
- using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on if you choose to do so.
